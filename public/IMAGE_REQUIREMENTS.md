# Image Requirements

## Required Images

Please add the following images to the `/public` folder:

### Hero Section
- **File**: `hero-image.jpg`
- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080px or larger
- **Description**: Main background image for the hero section
- **Style**: Romantic couple photo, landscape orientation

### Bride & Groom
- **File**: `bride.jpg`
- **Recommended Size**: 800x800px (square)
- **Description**: Portrait photo of the bride
- **Style**: Professional portrait, centered face

- **File**: `groom.jpg`
- **Recommended Size**: 800x800px (square)
- **Description**: Portrait photo of the groom
- **Style**: Professional portrait, centered face

### Gallery
Add 6 photos to the `gallery` folder:
- **Files**: 
  - `gallery/1.jpg`
  - `gallery/2.jpg`
  - `gallery/3.jpg`
  - `gallery/4.jpg`
  - `gallery/5.jpg`
  - `gallery/6.jpg`
- **Recommended Size**: 1000x1000px (square)
- **Description**: Various photos of the couple
- **Style**: Mix of formal and candid shots

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Quality**: High resolution for best results
- **File Size**: Optimize for web (under 500KB per image recommended)
- **Orientation**: 
  - Hero: Landscape
  - Portraits: Square
  - Gallery: Square

## Temporary Placeholders

Until you add real images, the site will show broken image icons. You can:
1. Use placeholder services like:
   - https://via.placeholder.com/800x800
   - https://picsum.photos/800/800
2. Or add your own placeholder images

## Tools for Image Optimization

- TinyPNG: https://tinypng.com/
- Squoosh: https://squoosh.app/
- ImageOptim: https://imageoptim.com/

## Next.js Image Component

All images use Next.js's optimized Image component, which:
- Automatically optimizes images
- Lazy loads images
- Serves responsive sizes
- Improves performance
